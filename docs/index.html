WHY ZENTRA?

Privacy by design

Everything runs locally. No data leaves your device. Your privacy is guaranteed by your own hardware.

Modular to the core

Every function is a plugin. Add, remove, or create your own. Customize your AI for your specific needs.

Cyberpunk ready

Integration with home automation, automotive, and robotics. Expand control over your physical environment without limits.

Dual-backend

Supports Ollama and KoboldCPP. Choose the model you want, including uncensored versions, without artificial restrictions.

CORE FEATURES

Total Control

Turn on lights, open doors, and command your PC with voice or structured text commands.

Autonomous Agentic Loop

Reasoning mode enabled. Zentra can break down complex tasks, search, and self-correct using a Chain of Thought visible in real-time.

Zentra Code Jail

Native AST-based Python sandbox. Zentra writes and executes code securely to solve math, logic, and data problems automatically.

Infinite Personalities

Load .txt anime profiles, change voice, and character. AI dynamically auto-syncs personalities without manual config.

Persistent Memory

Remembers conversations and learns from you thanks to a secure local SQLite archive.

Hardware Monitoring

CPU, RAM, and VRAM in real-time. Keep an eye on your local spaceship's performance.

Local Voice Synthesis

Integrated Piper TTS for clear, responsive, and completely offline voice interactions.

Bridge for Open WebUI

Use Zentra as the engine for advanced locally-maintained web interfaces.
