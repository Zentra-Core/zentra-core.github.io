Drive Media Viewer — Configuration

Integrated multimedia viewer for Zentra Drive. Supports images (JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, SVG), video (MP4, WebM, MKV), and audio (MP3, OGG, WAV, FLAC).

🖼️ Images
Show image metadata (EXIF)

Displays EXIF info: camera, date, GPS, etc.

Automatic Slideshow Mode

Starts the presentation automatically when entering an image folder.

🎬 Video
Autoplay video

Starts the video automatically upon opening (muted by browser policy).

Loop video

Repeats the video in loop when finished.

Show player controls

Shows play/pause, progress bar, volume and fullscreen.

🎵 Audio
Autoplay audio

Starts audio files automatically upon opening.

Show waveform visualizer

Shows animated waveform during audio playback.

🔧 General
Files with these extensions will open in the image viewer. Comma-separated.
Files with these extensions will open in the video player.
Files with these extensions will open in the audio player.
ℹ️ Formats and Compatibility
Playback depends on browser capabilities. For MKV and AVI, consider converting to MP4/WebM.
Media files are streamed from Drive (GET /api/drive/stream/<path>).
