ROOT CERTIFICATE (rootCA.pem)

Identifies this network as a 100% trusted source.

How to install on iPhone / iPad (iOS):
  1. Click the button above and "Allow" the "Configuration Profile" download.
  2. Open the Settings app on your iPhone. A "Profile Downloaded" message will appear at the top.
  3. Tap it and press Install (you will be asked for your phone's passcode).
  4. Go back to Settings > General > About > Certificate Trust Settings (at the bottom).
  5. Toggle the switch for Zentra Local Root CA to enable it.
  6. Close all Safari/Chrome tabs and reopen Zentra. You will have the green lock!
